## Key takeaways
- **Mega-losses moved off-chain** in 2026 (Kelp/rsETH $292M cross-chain config,
  Drift $285M governance social-eng). Not huntable as a code reviewer → these inform
  *target selection*, not review.
- **Code-level bounty bugs cluster** in: business logic (SC02), oracle (SC03),
  arithmetic/precision (SC07), then access control (SC01), reentrancy (SC08).
- **Audited ≠ safe** (Cetus, Balancer, Yearn). Tools verify "code does what it says",
  not "what it says is safe". That gap = the bounty.
- Code4rena is winding down; Immunefi absorbing. Code4rena's public reports are a
  free pattern goldmine to mine into our checklists.
